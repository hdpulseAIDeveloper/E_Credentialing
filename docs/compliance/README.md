## Audits and reviews

- NCQA CVO survey: every 3 years (next: TBD with Compliance)
- Internal policy review: annual
- Access audit: quarterly (user list, last-login, role justification)
- Penetration test: annual (third-party)
- Tabletop exercise (incident response): semi-annual
