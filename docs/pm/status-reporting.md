# Status Reporting

## Cadence

| Audience | Frequency | Owner | Channel |
|---|---|---|---|
| Engineering team | Daily standup | Eng Lead | Standup |
| Cross-functional core | Weekly Tuesday | PM | 30-min meeting + written summary |
| Sponsor + execs | Bi-weekly | PM | One-page email |
| Steering committee | Monthly | Sponsor | 60-min meeting + slides |
| External partners | Per release | Product | Release notes email |

## Bi-weekly executive update

A one-page email with: headlines, KPIs, top 3 risks, and the one ask of the
sponsor. Keep prose tight and link out for detail.

## Monthly steering deck

Slide outline (8 slides max):

1. Title + period.
2. KPI dashboard snapshot.
3. Wins (≤ 5 bullets).
4. In flight (≤ 5 bullets).
5. Risks & mitigations (≤ 5 rows).
6. Decisions needed.
7. Next month plan.
8. Q&A.
