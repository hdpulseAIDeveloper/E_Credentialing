# Manager Training Plan

*3 hours self-paced. Prerequisite: Specialist training.*

## Module 1 — Manager orientation (30 min)

**Read**
- [Credentialing workflow](../user/credentialing.md)
- [Reporting](../user/reporting.md)

**Practice**
1. Open the Pipeline report. Filter by specialist. Identify the specialist with the most "stuck" files.
2. Open the Turnaround report. Find the median time from invite to approval.

## Module 2 — Supervising specialists (45 min)

**Topics**
- Panel assignment and transfer
- Escalation: when and how to step in
- Performance coaching based on Pipeline and Turnaround reports
- Using Bulk Actions

**Practice**
1. Reassign a synthetic provider from one specialist to another with a reason.
2. Run a bulk action to send outreach reminders.
3. Override a specialist's decision on a document rejection. Observe the audit entry.

## Module 3 — Committee preparation (45 min)

**Read**
- [Committee workflow](../user/committee.md)

**Practice**
1. Generate an agenda for a synthetic committee meeting.
2. Review one provider's summary sheet. Identify gaps that would warrant a deferral.
3. After the meeting is finalized (sandbox fast-forwards the clock), open the minutes PDF.

## Module 4 — Compliance role (45 min)

**Read**
- [Reporting → Compliance](../user/reporting.md)
- [Sanctions](../user/sanctions.md)

**Practice**
1. Open the Compliance dashboard. Identify any red tile. Describe the remediation path.
2. Review a sanction flag and practice the "Confirm match" workflow (do not actually confirm in sandbox — just walk through the dialog).
3. Generate the auditor package and inspect its contents.

## Module 5 — Day in the life (15 min)

**Morning routine**
- Scan the Compliance dashboard for red/yellow
- Review overdue tasks across your team
- Triage urgent files

**Afternoon routine**
- Committee prep (on committee weeks)
- One-on-ones with specialists about their pipeline
- Quarterly reporting

## Competency check

Pass rate: 85% on a 15-question assessment covering: reporting, escalation workflow, committee preparation, compliance dashboard, sanction flag handling.
