# Provider Onboarding Guide

Welcome. This guide is for you — the healthcare provider being credentialed at Essen Medical. It walks you through completing your application using the ESSEN Credentialing Platform.

## What to expect

1. Essen sends you a secure invitation email.
2. You click the link, complete a 10-section application, and upload required documents.
3. Essen verifies your credentials with state boards, DEA, and other sources.
4. A credentialing committee reviews your file and approves you.
5. You receive a welcome message with next steps.

The whole process usually takes **30 to 60 days**, depending on how quickly external sources respond. You will receive progress emails throughout.

## Your invitation email

The email comes from **noreply@credentialing.hdpulseai.com** with the subject *Welcome to Essen Medical Credentialing*. It contains a secure sign-in link good for **72 hours**. If the link has expired, reply to the email or contact your Essen recruiter and they will send you a new one.

The link is tied to your email address and is single-use for each step. Do not forward it — anyone who opens it can continue your application.

## Completing the application

The application has 10 sections. You can save and return at any time.

| Section | What we ask for | About |
|---------|-----------------|-------|
| 1. Legal name and identity | Legal first / middle / last name, gender, date of birth, Social Security number | Required by federal law for credentialing. SSN is encrypted in our systems. |
| 2. Contact and address | Home address, mobile phone, personal email | We use your personal email as a backup if your Essen email is unavailable. |
| 3. Professional identifiers | NPI, DEA, CAQH ID, Medicare PTAN, Medicaid ID, ECFMG if applicable | Copy directly from your CAQH profile if possible. |
| 4. Education | Medical/graduate school name, country, graduation year | |
| 5. Training and residency | Internship, residency, and fellowship programs with dates | |
| 6. Work history | Every employer for the last 5 years, with start/end dates and reason for leaving | Gaps of more than 30 days need a short explanation. |
| 7. Professional references | 3 peer references with name, title, email, phone | They must be clinicians who have worked with you in the last 5 years. |
| 8. Licenses and certifications | Every active license — state medical licenses, DEA, board certifications, BLS/ACLS | Upload a clear photo or PDF of each. |
| 9. Hospital affiliations | Every hospital where you hold or have held privileges, with dates and status | Include the appointment letter if you have one. |
| 10. Attestation | Review everything; electronically sign | You must attest that every answer is true and complete. |

### Saving and returning

- The application saves automatically every 30 seconds as you type.
- You can close the browser and return later through the same invitation email.
- Until you submit, nothing goes to Essen — you can edit freely.

### Required documents

Your application cannot be submitted without these uploads:

- Photo ID (driver's license or passport)
- Social Security card
- Current CV or résumé
- Current malpractice insurance certificate
- Every license listed in section 8 (PDF or clear photo)
- Medical / graduate school diploma
- Board certification certificates (if section 3 lists any)
- BLS card (and ACLS/PALS if applicable)
- Infection control and child abuse certificates (New York)

### Tips for fast approval

- **Match CAQH exactly.** We ingest data from your CAQH profile; keeping CAQH current and matching your application to it prevents back-and-forth.
- **Upload legible documents.** Blurry photos cause rework. PDFs are best.
- **Use a real email for your references.** We will email them a short form; if the email bounces, it delays your approval.
- **Answer gaps honestly.** Any gap longer than 30 days needs a brief explanation. "Parental leave," "continuing education," and "relocation" are completely fine.

## What happens after you submit

### Primary Source Verification (PSV)

The platform runs automated verifications against:

- State medical boards (every state you hold a license in)
- DEA
- American Board of Medical Specialties and the applicable specialty board
- OIG and SAM.gov sanctions lists
- National Practitioner Data Bank (NPDB)
- Your medical / graduate school (AMA or ECFMG)

Most verifications complete automatically within 24 hours. If we cannot verify something automatically, a credentialing specialist will reach out to you.

### Reference and employment checks

We email your three professional references with a short form. We also email each employer from your work history to confirm dates. Let your references and former employers know to expect a message from **noreply@credentialing.hdpulseai.com**.

### Committee review

Once PSV is complete, your file goes to the next available credentialing committee meeting. Committees meet every other week. You will be notified by email after the committee votes.

### Approval and next steps

When you are approved, you receive:

- A formal welcome email.
- Information about your payer enrollments (Essen submits you to Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial payers).
- Orientation and training scheduling.

## Privacy and security

- Your data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored encrypted at rest in U.S. Azure datacenters.
- Social Security number, date of birth, home address, and home phone are encrypted with AES-256 before being written to the database.
- Only credentialing staff and committee members see your file. Nobody outside Essen can access it through the platform.
- Uploaded documents are stored in a private Azure Blob container; anyone downloading a file leaves an audit record.
- We never sell or share your data with third parties other than the payers you authorize enrollment with.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: I got a link but it says "invalid or expired."**
A: Links are valid for 72 hours. Reply to the invitation email or contact your Essen recruiter and they will send a fresh one.

**Q: I'm seeing an error while uploading a PDF.**
A: Make sure the file is under 50 MB and one of: PDF, JPG, PNG, TIFF. If the file is still failing, email it to your credentialing specialist and they can upload it for you.

**Q: My application went to the wrong email. Can you resend it?**
A: Yes — email us and we will reissue the invitation to the correct address. Do not share the original link with a colleague; it is tied to the original email.

**Q: How do I know where my application is in the process?**
A: You will receive an email at every major milestone (application received, PSV started, committee scheduled, decision made). Your recruiter can also check the status on your behalf at any time.

**Q: I already have a CAQH profile. Do I still have to fill out everything?**
A: We pre-fill most of your application from CAQH. You still need to review every field, sign the attestation, and upload any documents CAQH does not have.

## Who to contact

- Application problems: reply to your invitation email.
- Document or credential questions: your Essen recruiter or credentialing specialist.
- Urgent issues (deadline, missing email): call the credentialing office during business hours.
