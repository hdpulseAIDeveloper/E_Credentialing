# Getting Started

This page walks you through your first login, the main navigation, and the basics every staff user needs before diving into work.

## Before you start

You need:

- An Essen Microsoft 365 account (the platform signs you in with your Essen email).
- A role assigned by an Administrator. Ask your manager if you are not sure.
- The production URL: **https://credentialing.hdpulseai.com**

## First sign-in

1. Go to **https://credentialing.hdpulseai.com**.
2. Click **Sign in with Microsoft**. You will see the standard Essen sign-in page.
3. Complete your Microsoft multi-factor prompt (phone push, authenticator, etc.).
4. You land on the **Dashboard**. If you land on a permission denied page, your account does not have a credentialing role yet — ask an Administrator to assign one.

The first time you sign in, an Administrator may ask you to confirm your display name and contact phone.

## Your Dashboard

The Dashboard shows four sections:

- **Tasks assigned to me** — every work item that is yours today.
- **Providers I own** — providers you are the primary specialist for.
- **Soon to expire** — your providers with credentials expiring in the next 90 days.
- **Alerts** — flagged bot results, committee actions needed, audit items to review.

Click any card or row to open the full record.

## What a "provider" is

Everything in this platform revolves around a **Provider** — a clinician going through, or who has completed, credentialing at Essen. Each provider has a status that tracks where they are in the lifecycle:

| Status | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| Invited | We sent them an onboarding email; they have not started the application. |
| Onboarding in progress | They have opened the application and started filling it out. |
| Documents pending | They submitted the application; we are waiting on documents. |
| Verification in progress | PSV (license, DEA, board, sanctions, NPDB) is running or awaiting manual checks. |
| Committee ready | All verifications are complete and they are queued for a committee session. |
| Committee in review | A committee session has been scheduled and they are on the agenda. |
| Approved | The committee approved them. |
| Denied | The committee denied them. |
| Deferred | The committee asked for more information before voting. |
| Inactive | They are no longer active (left Essen, expired, etc.). |

## Keyboard shortcuts

Global shortcuts, available on any page:

| Keys | Action |
|------|--------|
| `/` | Focus the global search |
| `g p` | Go to Providers |
| `g d` | Go to Dashboard |
| `g c` | Go to Committee |
| `g e` | Go to Enrollments |
| `?` | Show the shortcut cheat sheet |
